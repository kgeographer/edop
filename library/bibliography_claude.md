# Cultural Landscape & Heritage Cities: Research Bibliography

## Core Theoretical Frameworks

### Carl Sauer & Cultural Geography
- Sauer, Carl O. (1925). *The Morphology of Landscape*. University of California Publications in Geography, 2(2), 19-53.
- Sauer, Carl O. (1941). *Foreword to Historical Geography*. Annals of the Association of American Geographers, 31(1), 1-24.
- Leighly, John (ed.) (1963). *Land and Life: A Selection from the Writings of Carl Ortwin Sauer*. Berkeley: University of California Press.

### Civilization Theory (for contrast/critique)
- Spengler, Oswald (1918-1922). *The Decline of the West* (Der Untergang des Abendlandes). [English trans. 1926-1928]
- Toynbee, Arnold J. (1934-1961). *A Study of History* (12 volumes). Oxford University Press.
- Huntington, Samuel P. (1996). *The Clash of Civilizations and the Remaking of World Order*. Simon & Schuster.
- Braudel, Fernand (1949/1972). *The Mediterranean and the Mediterranean World in the Age of Philip II*. University of California Press.
